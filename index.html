What is HackPOD?


HackPOD is a fast and powerful Arduino compatible development platform in a portable smartphone form factor. It is based on 32 bit ARM Cortex-M4 running at a clock speed of 72 MHz and features an integrated touchscreen LCD, in-built protoboard and SMT space, expandable memory , and a rechargeable battery.

    SMART FEATURES
    FOR PRO MAKERS


    • Build your prototypes and connect breakout modules and Arduino shields on the HackPOD protoboard itself.
    •  Plug and play support for SMT breakout modules such as RFM22B/23B.
    •  Hop an antenna on our on-board uFL mount for greater communication range .
    • HackPOD provides an on-board DAC pin. Use it as a signal generator, an oscilloscope or in many other ways.


    PLUG AND PLAY SUPPORT FOR ARDUINO BREAKOUT MODULES 

    • Adafruit. Sparkfun. Seeedstudio. Rhydolabs. Anything you name. Unless HackPOD runs out of "linear" pins it is 100% compatible. Just Plug 'n play.  Yes!! It's that simple.
    • You don't need any external breadboard!
    • Only development board that supports Electric Imp and nRF24L01 out-of-the-box!

    TECH SPECS


        32 bit ARM Cortex-M4 72 MHz MCU (overclockable 96 MHz)
        256K Flash Memory, 64K RAM, 2K EEPROM
        12 High Resolution Analog Inputs (16 Bit)
        19 Digital I/O Pins
        4 PWM outputs
        8 Timers for intervals/delays, separate from PWM
        USB with dedicated DMA memory transfers
        2 UARTs (serial ports)
    1 CAN BUS
        SPI, I2C, I2S, IR modulator
        I2S (for high quality audio interface)
        Real Time Clock (with user-added 32.768 crystal and battery)
        4 general purpose DMA channels (separate from USB)
        5 Touch Sensor Inputs
